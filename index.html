About This Experience Explore the beauty of Nuwara Eliya with a private local Tuk Tuk tour designed for travelers who want an authentic hill country experience. We provide friendly English-speaking local drivers with over 13 years of experience in tourism. During this 4.5–5 hour journey, we will take you to famous tea estates and factories including Bluefield, Kingswood, or Labookellie Tea Factory (depending on availability and guest preference). Guests can enjoy a traditional tea picking experience dressed like Sri Lankan tea pickers — perfect for memorable photos and a unique cultural experience. You can also visit the tea factory to see how world-famous Ceylon Tea is made, enjoy tea tasting sessions, and purchase fresh tea products. (Tea tasting may be free or payable depending on the factory.) Along the tour, we stop at beautiful viewpoints, hidden scenic spots, local markets, and popular landmarks around Nuwara Eliya and Ramboda. Adventure lovers can enjoy a 900-meter one-way hike to Ramboda Falls, and guests may swim in the natural pool if weather conditions are suitable. We provide pickup and drop-off from guest hotels and landmarks around Nuwara Eliya for a smooth and comfortable experience. Experience tea culture, nature, waterfalls, viewpoints, and local life — all in one unforgettable Tuk Tuk journey.

Safe rides, affordable prices and friendly local drivers.

Tea Picking Experience

Tea Tasting Experience

No visit to Nuwara Eliya is complete without tasting authentic world-famous Ceylon tea at its source. Explore a traditional tea factory, discover the journey from leaf to cup, and enjoy the rich aroma and fresh flavors of premium mountain-grown tea surrounded by breathtaking plantation views.

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Ramboda Falls surrounded by the misty mountains of Nuwara Eliya. Relax near crystal-clear natural pools, enjoy the fresh mountain air, and experience one of Sri Lanka’s most scenic nature escapes filled with waterfalls, greenery, and unforgettable views.
